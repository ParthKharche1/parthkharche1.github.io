‚öôÔ∏è Architecture: The ‚ÄúTwo-Stage‚Äù Approach

To achieve a massive 105.88:1 reduction ratio in a pancake form factor, I utilized a hybrid architecture:

  • Stage 1 (Pre-Stage): A Planetary Gearbox (4.235:1) handles the high-speed input. It reduces vibration and thermal buildup from the motor. Vibration and thermal buildup is undesirable at the output stage.
  • Stage 2 (Output): A Cycloidal Drive (25:1) handles the high-torque output. It provides shock-load resistance by distributing force across 30% of the pins simultaneously. Improves shock-load resistance and fatigue life.

  • Total Ratio: 105.88:1
  • Shortening Parameter (S): 0.591 (Optimized for smooth manufacturing and to balance smooth cycloidal motion).
  • Applications: Exoskeletons, Quadruped Legs, and Cobot Joints.
