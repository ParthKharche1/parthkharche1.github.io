Objective: Designed a high-performance swerve module featuring a custom planetary gear system for independent wheel rotation and steering.

Unlike standard modules, this design integrates a custom planetary gearbox for both steering and driving to optimize torque within a compact 140mm casing.

  • Drive System:

    • T-Motor U8 Pro (170 KV)
    • 1:7 planetary reduction for high-speed propulsion with sufficient torque for dynamic maneuvers

  • Steering System:

    • ClearPath servo motor
    • 1:10 planetary reduction for precise and backlash-minimized angular positioning

  • Planetary Gear Architecture:

    • Sun-driven planetary stage with enlarged planet gears to improve load distribution
    • Compact torque multiplication while maintaining coaxial alignment of drive and steering axes
    • Designed to reduce radial loads on motor shafts and bearings

The system uses a kinematic model-based control strategy to determine individual wheel vectors.

Omnidirectional Movement: Capable of translation and rotation simultaneously using inverse kinematics algorithms.
